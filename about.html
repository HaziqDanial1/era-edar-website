About Era Edar Marketing Sdn. Bhd.

Era Edar Marketing Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian company specializing in the marketing and distribution of health and wellness products.

The company offers a comprehensive range of health supplements, homeopathy products, and personal care items through our trusted nationwide direct selling network.

Committed to the principles of Halalan Thoiyiba, Era Edar ensures that all products are halal-certified, safe, and beneficial for consumers. Through our extensive network of branches and distributors across Malaysia, we support healthy lifestyles while empowering individuals with sustainable business and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Our Journey & Vision

Founded on the vision of providing pure and effective health alternatives, Era Edar has been a cornerstone of Malaysia's wellness industry for over 20 years. We take pride in being pioneers of biochemical and homeopathic products in the country.

Our journey is more than just business. It's a mission to bring 'Halalan Thoiyiba' awareness to every Malaysian household. We believe in empowering communities through health education and sustainable business opportunities.

Through innovations like Examo and our 'Wealth Through Health' concept, we're creating pathways for Bumiputera entrepreneurs to build successful businesses while contributing to national wellness goals.

Our Guiding Principles

Business

Era Edar Marketing Sdn. Bhd. upholds the spirit of economic jihad by proving that the Muslim community is capable of becoming competitive and successful entrepreneurs, both in this world and the hereafter.

Welfare

To fulfill its corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives, the company allocates a portion of its profits for infaq through the “Syarikat Perdekat Masyarakat” (SPM) program. The company also established the “Adopted School Program” to provide assistance and support to schools in rural areas.

Education

The company carries out activities focused on sharing knowledge related to health care and healthy nutrition based on the Halalan Thoiyibba concept with its members and the community. It also disseminates knowledge about the company’s products through talks and factory visits.
